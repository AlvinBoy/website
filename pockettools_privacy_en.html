Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

The Pocket Tools APP is a tool product. We highly respect your personal information and data, and will make every effort to ensure the security and reliability of your personal information and data. This privacy policy is formulated and disclosed in accordance with the relevant policies of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology.

1. Methods and Scope of Information Collection

  • 1.1 Obtaining Mobile Information: In order to uniquely identify a device, we will collect the hardware information of your current phone (including but not limited to OAID, IMEI, IMSI, MAC address, device serial number, BSSID, SSID) after you agree to the privacy policy. Third-party SDKs will also collect mobile information during initialization. The purpose is to analyze the usage of the app and prevent the same phone from repeatedly obtaining SMS verification codes in a short period of time.
  • 1.2 Third-Party SDK Disclosure: To provide professional and reliable services, these SDKs will only be initialized and run after the user agrees to the privacy policy. The third-party SDKs used in the app are described as follows:
    • Umeng Analytics SDK: Used for app operation statistics and analysis. It collects mobile hardware information (including but not limited to OAID, IMEI, IMSI, MAC address, device serial number, Android ID, BSSID, SSID).
  • 1.4 Complaints and Reporting: You can click on the "Feedback" section under "My" in the app to file complaints or report any personal information or other issues you encounter.
